Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Edurena uses very few cookies. We do not track you for advertising. We do not build profiles. Cookies on this platform exist only to keep you logged in and to allow third-party services like Paystack to process payments securely.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They can store session identifiers, preferences, and other small amounts of data. Some cookies are set by the website itself; others are set by third-party services embedded in the page.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
sb-auth-token Essential Keeps you logged in to your school or teacher account via Supabase authentication Session / 7 days
sb-refresh-token Essential Refreshes your login session without requiring you to sign in again 7 days

These cookies are strictly necessary for the platform to work. Without them, you cannot stay logged in. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Certain features load content from external services, which may set their own cookies:

  • Paystack — When you visit the Pricing or checkout page, Paystack's script may set cookies to support secure payment processing and fraud prevention. These are governed by Paystack's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may set a short-lived cookie or log your IP as part of the font request. This is a standard web practice and does not result in persistent tracking.

We have no control over the cookies set by these third parties. If you are concerned, you can block third-party cookies in your browser settings — the core Edurena platform will still function.

4. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, the platform uses your browser's local storage to hold temporary session data (such as your current exam state or dashboard preferences). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us outside of normal API calls.

5. How to Control Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most modern browsers let you:

  • View all cookies stored by a website
  • Block cookies from specific sites or all third-party sites
  • Delete all cookies when you close the browser

Blocking the essential session cookies (listed in Section 2) will prevent you from staying logged in to the platform.

Browser-specific instructions: Chrome · Firefox · Safari · Edge

6. Changes to This Policy

If we add new cookies or change how we use existing ones, we will update this page and the date at the top. We will not add advertising or tracking cookies without clearly notifying active users first.

7. Contact

For any questions about cookies or your privacy, contact us at hello@edurena.online.
